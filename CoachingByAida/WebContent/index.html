Coaching

What is Coaching?

Coaching is about getting the very best out of you and enabling you to make decisions that will improve your life.
'Coaching' can be applied virtually anywhere where people are trained, developed and/or helped towards improving performance, and achieving success and personal fulfillment.

Coaching is a quite recent area of learning and development. Coaching commonly addresses attitudes, behaviors, knowledge, as well as skills.

Coaching is a form of change facilitation - it enables people, rather than trains them.
Coaching is interactive and flexible - it allows for personal transition on an individual basis.
Coaching makes no assumptions - it's not judgmental, nor is it prescriptive or instructional.

Coaching principles:

  • Everyone is capable of achieving more
  • Your past is no indication of your future
  • Your beliefs about what is possible for you are your only limits
  • A coach always provides full support
  • Coaches don't provide the answers, they lead you to find the answers
  • Coaching does not include criticizing you
  • All coaching is completely confidential

Coaching can be conducted wherever coach and client happen to be - anywhere in the world. Coaching can be conducted via mail, as well. Coaching is just as effective over the telephone/cell phone, skype, as it is face-to-face.. This offers greater flexibility for people with a busy lifestyle.

As for the coaching teams, coaching is organized on the premises of the company or where company decides to perform coaching.
